Delcastle Drama Club

Welcome to Drama Club!

Drama Club is a Delcastle group that works to preform two shows each year.
Throughout the year,
we preform a play in the winter, and a musical in the spring.
You can join as either an actor,
part of the stage crew, or part of the lighting crew.
