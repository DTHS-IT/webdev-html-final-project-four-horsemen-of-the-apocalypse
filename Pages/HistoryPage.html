When Drama Started...

Before the Delcastle Players had a formal "Drama Club", there was the Theatre Arts Shop!
The Theatre Arts Shop has the same purpose as the Drama Club it just didn't take place outside of school
and you'd be able to make props and fully learn how to be a good actor during your shop hours.
This program ended in the late 1990's and Drama Club took it's place.

How Long It's Been Running...

We don't know the exact year when Drama Club started,
but considering the fact that it began after the Theatre Arts Shop was removed,
a good estimate would be the Delcastle Player's Drama Club has been operating for at last 30 years!
