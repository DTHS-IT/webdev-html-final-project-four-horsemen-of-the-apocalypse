What do we do in Drama Club?

In Drama Club we perform one play in the winter and one musical in the spring.
Throughout the process of putting on the show we practice two times a week and perfect our craft.
Drama Club is a great way to find new friends and to try something new.
No experience is required to become a member of the Delcastle Players.


As a member of the Delcastle Players, you can:

  • Be an actor
    (When you are an actor you have the opportunity on stage in our productions.
    Being an actor offers you an opportunity to showcase your abilities on stage while also learning how to become a better actor and you can learn social skills by interacting with others)
  • Be a part of our stage crew
    (When you are a part of our stage crew you can help out with building the set pieces,
    helping out with set changes, and helping out with props.)
  • Be a part of our lighting crew
    (When you are a part of our lighting crew you will learn how to operate the lighting board and or spotlights.)
