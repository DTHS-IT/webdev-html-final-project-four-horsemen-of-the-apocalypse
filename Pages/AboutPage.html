What do we do in Drama Club?

In Drama Club we preform one play in the winter and one musical in the spring. Throughout the process of putting on the show we practice two times a week and prefect our craft. Drama Club is a great way to find new friends and to try something new. No experience is required to become a member of the Delcastle Players.


As a member of the Delcastle Players you can:

  • Become an actor (When you are an acotr you have the opportunity on stage in our productions.)
  • Be a part of our stage crew (When you are a part of our stage crew you can help out with building the set peices, helping out with set changes, and helping out with props.)
  • Be a part of our lighting crew (When you are a part of our lighting crew you will learn how to opperate the lighting board and or spotlights.)
